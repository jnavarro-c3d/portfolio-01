About Me

My name is Josiah Emmanuel Navarro. I am a 24-year old college student who will be graduating from the Colorado School of Mines after the 2018 Spring semester (May 2018). I will be graduating with a Bachelor's Degree in Computer Science. While my experience programming and exercising software engineering practices began at Mines, it wasn't long before I developed a passion for creating by speaking the language of computers (so to speak).

Alongside pursuing my career as a software engineer/front-end web developer, my second passion lies in church ministry. My faith is something that I hold near and dear, and it is something that I tend to openly share with others. I currently serve as the worship ministry director and the youth ministry director at CrossWind Church of Denver. I have been serving at CrossWind Church for over 5 years now.
